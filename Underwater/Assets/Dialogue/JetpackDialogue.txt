Plebeians swim. Great men use Triton Corp’s Aquatic Thrust Pack (ATP). 
Traverse the seven seas in style with this simple and easy-to-use control scheme!

While underwater:
1. Hit the Z key once on your diving suit's command board for a short vertical boost.
2. While "airborne," hold a directional key then hit the Z key again for a boost in the indicated direction.
3. Hold down the Z key on the second boost for added distance.
